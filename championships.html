Thames Punting Championships 2017

The TPC Regatta 2017 will take place from 10am - 4:30pm on Sunday 6th August. For information on entries and further details visit our Facebook page.

Directions

The Punting Championships takes place on the east bank of the Thames at Maidenhead, just downstream of Brunel's famous railway bridge.
