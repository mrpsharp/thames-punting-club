Where to go Punting

If you are interested in punting you could join a club or visit one of the following locations.

Punt hire

Bath Boating Station

Bath Boating Station, Forester Rd, Bath BA2 6QE (they have nice old skiffs, wooden canoes, and a restaurant upstairs; plus a couple of rooms to rent).

Scudamore's Cambridge

Punting in Cambridge both on the Cam and the Granta.

Magdelen Bridge Boathouse

Punting on Oxford's River Cherwell.
